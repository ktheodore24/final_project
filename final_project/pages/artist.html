Mariah Angeliq: La Princesa de Miami

Mariah Angeliq is a rising star in urban Latin music. With over a billion streams and over a million monthly listeners on Spotify, the Miami native's career has catapulted within only a few years. It all started with her first smash hit "Perreito", which has amassed millions of listens and views across all streaming services and YouTube, respectively, to date. This isn't a surprise, considering the fact that Mariah has always bet on herself and knew she was destined for stardom before anyone else.
